Mango Banana Smoothie

Description

This Mango Banana Smoothie is a creamy, nutritious drink that combines the sweetness of ripe mango and banana with the richness of milk and Greek yogurt. The addition of cinnamon brings a warm, aromatic flavor that enhances the tropical taste of the fruit. Packed with vitamins, fiber, protein, and probiotics from the yogurt, this smoothie is perfect for breakfast, a snack, or a post-workout boost. It’s a satisfying and healthy treat that’s both refreshing and filling, perfect for any time of the day!

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe mango (peeled and diced)
  • 1 ripe banana (peeled)
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup plain Greek yogurt (or butter)
  • A pinch of cinnamon
  • Ice cubes (for a cooler, thicker smoothie)

Steps:

  1. Peel and dice the mango, and peel the banana.
  2. Add the mango, banana, milk, Greek yogurt,cinnamon, and ice cubes to a blender.
  3. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  4. Pour into a glass and serve immediately.
