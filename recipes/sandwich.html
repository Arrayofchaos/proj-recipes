Avo Sandwich

Description

This Avocado Sandwich with Eggs is a simple yet satisfying meal, perfect for breakfast or lunch. The creamy avocado spread adds a rich, smooth texture that complements the runny yolk of a perfectly cooked egg. Spread generously on toasted whole grain bread, this sandwich is both nutritious and delicious. With the optional kick from chili flakes or hot sauce, and a touch of lemon juice to balance the flavors, each bite offers a combination of creamy, savory, and slightly tangy notes. Quick to prepare and packed with healthy fats and protein, this sandwich will leave you feeling full and energized.

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of whole grain bread (or your preferred bread)
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil (or butter)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice (optional)
  • A pinch of chili flakes or hot sauce (optional, for some spice)
  • Fresh lettuce leaves
  • 2 slices of tomato
  • Fresh herbs (like parsley, cilantro, or chives) for garnish (optional)

Steps:

  1. Mash the avocado with salt, pepper, and lemon juice.
  2. Toast the bread slices.
  3. Heat the olive oil or butter in a pan over medium heat. Crack the eggs into the pan and cook them to your desired level.
  4. Spread the mashed avocado generously on one slice of the toasted bread.Layer with lettuce, tomato slices, and the cooked egg. Top with the second slice of toasted bread.
  5. Cut the sandwich in half and garnish with fresh herbs, if desired. Serve immediately and enjoy!
